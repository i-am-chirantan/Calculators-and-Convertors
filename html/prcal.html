Profitability Ratios Calculator

This calculator will provide answers for up to four measures of a company's or organization's profitability: return on assets, return on equity, gross profit margin, operating profit margin, net profit margin, earnings per share, and price/earnings (P/E) ratio. The calculator can only calculate one or two sets of data points and will only return results for ratios that can be calculated based on the user's inputs.
